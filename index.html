Introduction

Zipack is an efficient serialization format, it's a replacement to JSON, but smaller, faster and richer with data types including Number, String, Bool, Null, ByteArray, List, Map and reserved types. Of cause zipack is schema-less. In depth, zipack borrows ideas from Huffman Encoding, MessagePack, Protocol Buffer, Git's VLQ, etc. but defines a better compression algorithm than all of them .
